December monthly meeting

Organized by Igal Koshevoy

Time: Tuesday, December 4, 2007 from 6–8pm

Location: CubeSpace, CubeSpace, 622 SE Grand, Portland, OR

Agenda

The next Portland Ruby Brigade meeting is right around the corner.

Are you working on something interesting in Ruby? Did you come across a cool new tool? Have a tricky question? Maybe you’d like to talk about RedMine, Retrospectiva and their discoveries from your quest for a web-based project management tool? Or talk about the various CMS and blogging tools for Ruby, and where they’re useful? Or something else?

Please get your ideas together soon because we’d be glad to hear more about them at the meeting.

I can do a talk on simplifying Ruby web development with Haml, Sass, RedCloth and StaticMatic.

Hope to see you all soon.

-igal

PS: I’ll make sure this time that presenters are speaking towards the audience and are loud enough that everyone can hear.
