Group business meeting regarding planning and organization

Organized by Igal Koshevoy

Time: Monday, April 7, 2008 from 7–9pm

Location: Green Dragon Bistro & Brew Pub, 928 Se 9th Ave Portland, OR 97214 (503) 517-0606

Agenda

At tonight’s meeting we discussed having a separate business meeting to deal with various meta-issues like fixing up the catherder (our website), recruiting speakers, getting started on FOSCON planning, and other organizational concerns. If you’re interested in participating, we’ll be getting together at the Green Dragon (928 SE 9th Ave) at 7pm on Monday, April 7. We’ll start plotting some of the fun and interesting things the group would like to be doing, and of course drink beer.
