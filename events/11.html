Hackfest 3.1: Camping!

Organized by Lennon Day-Reynolds

Time: Thursday, June 15, 2006 from 6–10pm

Location: Ugly Mug Coffee, 8017 SE 13th Ave. Portland, OR 97202

Agenda

We’ll work on the “Cat Herder” re-write in Camping, based on work begun by Lennon (a.k.a. rcoder) with an eye towards potentially replacing or supplementing the current system.

Bring a laptop if you can; we’ll be working with the following development environment (all available via Rubygems):

  • Camping (stable gem is fine)
  • Markaby (use the ‘markaby-careful’ branch from _why’s SVN repos, or the pre-built gem rcoder built)
  • Mongrel (optional, but way faster than WEBrick)
  • SQLite3 (see _why’s instructions)
  • Mosquito (only available via SVN from topfunky)
