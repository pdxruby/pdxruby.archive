PoDruRuTu

Organized by Lennon Day-Reynolds

Time: Tuesday, December 12, 2006 from 6–9pm

Location: Acme, 1305 SE 8th Ave. Portland, OR 97214

Agenda

That’s right, kids and cadets…it’s the return of PoDruRuTu! (that’s Portland Drunken Ruby Tuesday, BTW)

This time, we’ll be trying a new venue for PDX.rb — the lovely Acme Food & Drink, just a few blocks from our usual haunts, but with a slightly more varied food, beer, and cocktail menu.

So, if you’ve got a project you want some in-person review for, or feel like rolling up your sleeves and hacking on the website for a bit, come on by!
