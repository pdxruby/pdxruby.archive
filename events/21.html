Dec. Meeting

Organized by Lennon Day-Reynolds

Time: Tuesday, December 5, 2006 from 6–9pm

Location: FreeGeek, 1731 SE 10th Ave. Portland, OR 97214

Agenda

It’s time to update the website — there are at least four different sub-sites (event scheduler, blog, wiki, mailing lists) that have gotten wildly out of sync/broken, and the recent Planet Argon outage may have been the last straw for at least some of them.

So, anyone interested in contributing to the next generation of the pdx.rb online presence should come on down! (Location to be confirmed soon, but it’s probably safe to assume we’ll be at FreeGeek barring some major snafu.)
