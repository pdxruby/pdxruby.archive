Open Source Bridge conference

Organized by Igal Koshevoy

Time: Wednesday, June 17, 2009 at 9am through Friday, June 19, 2009 at 9pm

Location: Oregon Convention Center, 777 NE Martin Luther King, Jr. Blvd. Portland, Oregon 97232

Agenda

Open Source Bridge is a new conference for developers working with open source technologies and for people interested in learning the open source way. The conference features talks on Ruby and other technologies. Learn more at http://opensourcebridge.org/

This conference is organized and supported by many members of the Portland Ruby Brigade and replaces our yearly FOSCON event.
