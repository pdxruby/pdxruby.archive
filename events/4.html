Monthly Meeting

Organized by Caleb Phillips

Time: Tuesday, April 4, 2006 from 7–9pm

Location: FreeGeek, 1731 SE 10th Ave. Portland, OR 97214

Agenda

  • FOSCON with input and organization from John
  • Some other stuff.

Minutes

We decide that today’s (non) agenda isn’t really
conducive to podcasting.

FOSCON (led by John)

  • John says that he got in touch with an O’Reilly contact
    and they are positive about the idea of another FOSCON.
  • Eric says that he has also been in touch with O’Reilly and
    they have offered some space for groups like PerlMongers and
    PDX.Ruby.
  • John proposes setting up a group of interested people to meet
    monthly before meetings (maybe an hour) and work on FOSCON. A few
    people suggest that they are interested in this.
  • Difference between FOSCON (presumably at freegeek) and at-convention stuff? Suggestion of FOSCON as a party, and at-convention stuff is more formal to show people about ruby and about Portland, perhaps some hackfest type stuff. Want to avoid conflicts with other peoples parties’. Wednesday or later. Wednesday seems best becaust it is similar to last time (i.e. before many presenters present)

Hackfest

  • Hackfest for website and other stuff. Decide on Tuesday, April 11th
    at Lucky Lab at 7pm. About 5 people hope to do work on the website, others will work on random projects.
  • Eric suggests that Saturday morning hackfests may be more productive,
    we will look at a Saturday Morning hackfest if the Tuesday Night seems
    not-productive-enough.

Quizzes

  • Ben wants to do ruby quizzes. People show interest, so he is going
    to set that up.
