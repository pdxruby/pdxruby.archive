Portland Ruby Brigade monthly meeting

Organized by Igal Koshevoy

Time: Tuesday, June 5, 2012 from 7–9pm

Location: CrowdCompass office in the Ford Building, 2505 SE 11th Ave, #300 Portland, OR 97202, US ACCESS NOTES: Suite 300, 3rd floor. Office will have a CrowdCompass sign. Enter the building after hours on Division by walking through Ford Food & Drink, or on 11th by walking through the parking lot.

Agenda

Join other developers for presentations and discussions about Ruby and its uses. If you have a Ruby-related talk, discussion topic or question, please suggest it on the mailing list.

ABOUT: The Portland Ruby Brigade is a user group for Ruby programmers in the Portland, Oregon, area. Join other developers for presentations and discussions about Ruby, libraries, tools and techniques. The group usually meets on the first Tuesday of the month, “Ruby Tuesday”.

VENUE: This venue is kindly provided by CrowdCompass, “the ultimate mobile app provider for conferences”: http://www.crowdcompass.com/
