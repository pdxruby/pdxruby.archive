RSpec & Rubinius at 3/6 Meeting

Organized by Brian Ford

Time: Tuesday, March 6, 2007 from 6:15–6:45pm

Location: FreeGeek, 1731 SE 10th Ave. Portland, OR 97214

Agenda

I will give a brief introduction to RSpec, along with a tad of Behavior-driven development (BDD). I will show examples of using RSpec with Rails and Rubinius. And to give that some context, I will give a very brief intro to Rubinius as well.
