Hackfest

Organized by Caleb Phillips

Time: Tuesday, April 11, 2006 from 7–10pm

Location: The Lucky Lab, 915 SE Hawthorne Blvd. Portland, OR 97214

Agenda

  • Hacking on pdxruby.org
  • Creating of subversion accounts for those interested in working on pdxruby.org
  • Hacking on ruby stuff in general
  • Drinking of beer

Minutes

None of the maybes showed, but all three of the yesses did. We drank some beer and hacked on the www.pdxruby.org website code (a.k.a. cat-herder). In the process we fixed a couple outstanding bugs and made a couple small improvements. We also presumptuously pushed our changes out to the production site, so now this site is running the latest.
